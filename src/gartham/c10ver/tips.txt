You can get daily, weekly, and monthly rewards with the commands: `~daily`, `~weekly`, and `~monthly` respectively!
Every time you send a message in #general, there's a small chance you'll stumble upon some loot.
You can open crates using the `open crate` command! Just type `~open crate crate-type`.
You can pay other users using the `pay` command!
Eating food will give you a temporary multiplier. You can eat food with `~use food-name`.
Wanna support us? Check out the official store (http://clover.gartham.com/store )!
You can buy color roles using the `~color` command!
Low on funds? Start a Math lobby with `~math` and get cloves for doing math!
